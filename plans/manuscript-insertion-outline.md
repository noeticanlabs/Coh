# Manuscript insertion outline for V1 to V2 continuity

## Target manuscript

- [`coh-category-zenodo-final/paper/main.tex`](../coh-category-zenodo-final/paper/main.tex)

## Objective

Make the paper explicitly state that V2 is not a replacement theory but the projection-aware categorical completion of the V1 operational framework.

## Insertion 1: end of Introduction

### Location

Insert near the end of [`Introduction`](../coh-category-zenodo-final/paper/main.tex:73), after the contribution list around [`lines 88-94`](../coh-category-zenodo-final/paper/main.tex:88).

### Purpose

State the architectural relationship before the reader encounters the later hidden/observable abstraction.

### Content to insert

Add a short paragraph of the form:

> The present development should be read in two layers. The first is an operational layer of certified execution traces, verifier acceptance, and exact bookkeeping. The second is a projection-aware abstract layer built from the first: hidden realizations, observable traces, fibers, semantic envelope, and certified categorical closure. In this sense, the abstract categorical theory is not a separate replacement for the execution model, but its projection-aware completion.

### Effect

This prevents the reader from interpreting the later semantic-cost and category constructions as a change of theory.
